About Neura

Ensuring projects are completed to your exact specifications.

Whether you need to install a new air conditioning system, repair a faulty wiring system, or replace a damaged transformer, we have the expertise and resources to get the job done.

We offer a full range of rewiring services, from new installations to upgrades and repairs. We understand the importance of safety, and our services are designed to ensure the integrity of your electrical system.

What We Do

Services we provide

01
Lighting & Powerpoints

Comprehensive lighting and powerpoint services for both commercial and residential needs — from architectural downlights to feature pendant wiring.

02
Switchboards

Professional switchboard rewiring, installation of new switchboards, testing, certification and safety advice. All work to AS/NZS standards.

03
Emergency Callouts

Our experienced electricians are on call 24/7 and can respond to any electrical emergency. Fault finding, repair and restore — fast.

04
Smoke Alarms

Full inspection, testing and certification of all smoke alarms. We ensure correct operation and replace any defective components to keep you safe.

05
CCTV Installation

Specialising in CCTV installation services. Our team installs a wide variety of CCTV and security systems tailored to your needs.

06
Data & Communications

Structured cabling, NBN, access control and intercom systems fully integrated into new builds, fitouts and high-rise developments.
